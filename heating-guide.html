Stovetop Heating

Remove the meal from its container and heat it in a pot over medium heat. Stir regularly.

If the sauce becomes too thick, add warm water a little at a time until it reaches the desired consistency.

Ensure the food is steaming hot before serving.

Microwave Heating

  • Thawed meals: dish into a microwave-safe plate and heat until hot.
  • Rice: heat in an open container on high for 3 minutes.
  • Mashed potatoes or pasta: microwave on high for 2 minutes.
